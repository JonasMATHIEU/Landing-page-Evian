About Evian

Evian is a French brand of mineral water products. It is known for its pure and high quality natural mineral water from the Evian lake in the French Alps. Since its creation in 1826, the brand has established itself as a world leader in the bottled water market. Evian offers a wide range of mineral water products, such as bottled still and sparkling mineral water, as well as skin and baby wellness products. The brand is committed to sustainable initiatives, such as reducing its ecological footprint and promoting a healthy lifestyle. Evian strives to provide its consumers with superior quality products, while respecting the environment and working with local partners. The brand is present in more than 140 countries around the world and is appreciated for its upscale lifestyle and commitment to quality and sustainability.

As adults, our body consists of about 60% water. It is essential to maintain this balance because water is an essential element for its proper functioning. A hydrated body thus ensures normal physical and cognitive functions*. There are many options for drinks. But water is one of the best sources of hydration possible. And with Evian natural mineral water, nature offers us an ideal hydration: pure water and 100% natural**. At evian we firmly believe that it is important to accompany you to develop healthier hydration habits, that is, drink enough water every day. This water intake is made by what we drink (80% of the water we consume comes from what we drink) and our diet (20% of the water we consume comes from what we eat). The European Food Safety Agency (EFSA) has established guidelines on the amount of water to be consumed daily according to age, sex, pregnancy or breastfeeding.
